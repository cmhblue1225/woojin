As a field that acquires knowledge and skills related to computers, such as computer hardware, software, multimedia, and embedded systems, and applies them to various areas, our students will learn programming languages, networks, computer system operating systems, and artificial intelligence. Students also acquire various application abilities based on basic computer knowledge and develop leading talents to innovatively create new fields that combine diverse expertise and technologies in computer science. Furthermore, we educate global talents to become competitive in the international community. Career paths after graduation Internet development and management companies, smart system developers, information security companies, IT consultants, government agencies, financial institutions, research institutes, software solution companies, system integrators, game and content solution developers, and video, audio, and media companies.\nMajor in Smart Mobility\nTo acquire the creative knowledge skills required in the 4th industrial revolution era, we train a creative workforce who can learn the hardware and software fields of IT and converge with other fields. Students will learn to produce hardware and write software in IT, acquire interdisciplinary knowledge between IT technology and other majors, and develop creative works. Career paths after graduation Government offices, research centers, IT, telecommunications, mobile, electronics manufacturers, SI, etc.\nMajor in AI Bigdata\nAs a major that learns the core technologies and theories required in the future society of the 4th industrial revolution, students cultivate technical skills to maximize industrial efficiency by understanding, processing, analyzing, and predicting various types of data accumulated in all industries. Career paths after graduation Students can proceed to various industries through the development of big data and AI convergence SW required by society.
